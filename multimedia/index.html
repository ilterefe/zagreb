Zagreb Glavni kolodvor

Zagreb Glavni kolodvor is the main railway station in Zagreb. Located 1 km south of the city's main square, it is the largest station in Croatia and the main hub of the Croatian Railways network

Sava River

The Sava River is a river in Central and Southeastern Europe, a right tributary of the Danube. It flows through Slovenia, Croatia, along the northern border of Bosnia and Herzegovina, and through Serbia, discharging into the Danube in Belgrade.
